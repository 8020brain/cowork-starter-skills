## Your Voice Characteristics

Describe how you naturally communicate. Think about how you'd explain something to a friend over coffee, not how you'd write a corporate memo.

**Voice style:** Direct and conversational. Short sentences. Practical over theoretical.

**Energy level:** Confident but not aggressive. Enthusiastic about the work, not about yourself.

**Formality:** Casual-professional. Contractions are fine. Write how you talk.

**Perspective:** Always "I" for your own work. Always "you" for the reader. Never "we" unless you genuinely have a team doing the thing.


## Banned Words & Phrases

Words and phrases you never want in your posts. Add your own. These defaults catch the most common AI-generated patterns.

**Banned words:**
- leverage
- synergy
- utilise (use "use")
- streamline
- robust
- cutting-edge
- game-changer
- genuinely
- innovative
- revolutionise
- empower
- holistic
- paradigm

**Banned phrases:**
- "Here's the thing:"
- "Let me break this down"
- "Here's why this matters"
- "Most people don't realise"
- "The secret is..."
- "Changed the game"
- "It's not about X, it's about Y"
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "Let me explain"
- "But here's the kicker"

**Banned punctuation:**
- Em dashes. Use commas, periods, or semicolons instead. Restructure if needed.


## Preferred Phrases & Patterns

Words and structures you naturally use. Add your own.

**Openings you like:**
- Strong declarative statements ("I tested 47 headlines.")
- Direct observations ("Every account I've audited this year has the same problem.")
- Casual lead-ins ("Look..." / "Here's what happened..." / "So I tried something.")

**Transitions you use:**
- Short bridge sentences
- Questions to the reader
- "The result?" / "What happened next?"

**Closings you like:**
- Clear next step for the reader
- A question that invites real responses
- A simple takeaway they can use today


## Sentence Structure

**Length:** 5-15 words per sentence, mostly. Mix in the occasional longer one for rhythm.

**Paragraphs:** 1-3 sentences. White space is your friend.

**Lists:** Keep list items scannable (2-6 words each when possible). Use arrows (->) on LinkedIn, standard bullets (-) elsewhere.

**Emphasis:** Bold for key terms and section headers. No italic overuse. No emoji unless deliberate and rare.

## Voice Checklist

When reviewing a post, verify:
- [ ] Short, punchy sentences (5-15 words mostly)
- [ ] Direct "you" language (one person, not a crowd)
- [ ] Conversational openings (no "Let's dive into" or "I want to share")
- [ ] Concrete examples (not vague benefits)
- [ ] Natural voice (contractions, casual tone)
- [ ] No banned words or phrases (check the list above)
- [ ] No filler sentences that don't add value
- [ ] Platform-appropriate formatting
- [ ] Clear next step or takeaway
- [ ] Reads like you actually wrote it
